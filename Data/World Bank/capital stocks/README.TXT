Nehru, Vikram, and Ashok Dhareshwar. 1993. "A New Database on
Physical Capital Stock: Sources, Methodology and Results." Rivista de Analisis Economico 8 (1): 37-59


This file contains the data set compiled by Vikram Nehru and Ashok Dhareshwar 
in the paper A New Database on Physical Capital Stock: Sources, Methodology and 
Results.
The data is annual, covers the period 1950 to 1990, and is grouped in one file provided 
both in lotus (".wk1") and text (".txt") format.
The variables included in the data set are:

Total physical capital
GDP at market prices
Human capital stock: mean years of primary education
Human capital stock: mean years of secondary education
Human capital stock: mean years of tertiary education
Human capital stock: total mean years education
Gross dom. fixed investment
GDFI in producers durables (share of total GDFI)
Potential GDP series
Total mid-year population
Population between ages 15 and 64
